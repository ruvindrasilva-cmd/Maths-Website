Ruvindra Silva

Specialist in Pearson Edexcel iAL Mathematics

With over a decade of experience in teaching A-Level Mathematics, Ruvindra Silva has helped hundreds of students achieve top grades in their Edexcel iAL exams. His teaching methodology focuses on building a strong conceptual foundation while mastering the specific techniques required for exam success.

Ruvindra believes that mathematics is not just about memorizing formulas, but about understanding the logic and beauty behind them. His classes are interactive, challenging, and designed to foster critical thinking skills that go beyond the classroom.
